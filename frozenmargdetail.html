Frozen Margaritas 🍹

5 stars (⭐⭐⭐⭐)

Ingredients

Amount Food item
3/4 cup Silver tequila
1/2 cup Fresh lime juice (from 4 to 6 limes)
1/2 cup Triple sec or cointreau
1/2 ounce Simple syrup
4 cups Ice

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the tequila, lime juice, triple sec, simple syrup and ice. Blend until smooth
  2. If desired, rub a wedge around the outer rim of four glasses and roll in salt or sugar
  3. Pour in margarita mixture and serve, garnished with lime slices
